Our Mission Against Drugs

The Kerala Police Department is committed to combating drug abuse and trafficking throughout the state. Our comprehensive approach includes enforcement, prevention, education, and rehabilitation.

We work closely with schools, communities, and healthcare providers to create awareness about the dangers of drug use and to provide support to those affected by addiction.

Our Journey Against Drugs

The key milestones in our fight against drug abuse in Kerala.

2018

Campaign Launch

Kerala Police launched the "Say No To Drugs" campaign to combat rising drug abuse cases.

2019

School Outreach Program

Expanded awareness programs to 500+ schools across Kerala, reaching over 100,000 students.

2021

Rehabilitation Partnership

Partnered with 50+ rehabilitation centers to provide support for recovery and reintegration.

2023

Digital Campaign

Launched online resources and reporting tools to make help more accessible.

2025

Drug-Free Kerala Vision

Working towards our goal of making Kerala completely drug-free by 2030.
